Why Blue?

Our first customers were the band Blue Man Group. They had one requirement, the houses needed to be blue. This was a tough task to take on but with our combined skills we achieved it in record time with little effort. This made us realise that there was a possible market for blue houses and something that other companies were not exploring. If the house you really want is not blue then we provide free painters to make it so. The sky is the limit!
