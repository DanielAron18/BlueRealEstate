About Us

Welcome to Blue Real Estate, where exemplary service and fast sales are a priority! This company was established in 2019 by five guys who have lived and breathed the real estate industry for many years now. We worked for other companies in the past but decided to join together and create our own path in the real estate world. This is a young company, but we strive for big things in the near future and are already making great strides in the markets today.

Why blue?

Our first customers were the band Blue Man Group. They had one requirement, the houses needed to be blue. This was a tough task to take on but with our combined skills we achieved it in record time with little effort. This made us realise that there was a possible market for blue houses and something that other companies were not exploring. If the house you really want is not blue then we provide free painters to make it so. The sky is the limit!

If you’re in need of a new home then we are confident that we can find your dream BLUE home at a reasonable price, with emphasis on blue! The sky is blue so why shouldn’t your house be as well?
